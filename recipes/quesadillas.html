Mexican Quesadillas

Home

In this image we can see a plate full of mouth-watering quesadillas

Ingredients:

  • Cheese
  • Flour tortillas
  • Salsa
  • Pico de Gallo

Steps:

  1. Heat up the tortillas
  2. Add the cheese so it melts while heating the tortilla
  3. Put all the ingredients into a tortilla
  4. Enjoy!
